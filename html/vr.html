What is virtual reality?

Virtual reality is a way of fooling the senses to believe that a different reality is true. In it's simplest form it can even be watching ASMR videos with your eyes closed, feeling like you are in a different place due to the audio-sensory input while lacking visual stimuli that contrasts. More advanced virtual reality systems include headsets and headphones that project images and noises of a different world.
